Application Areas

Spiral-welded pipes are used in various industries, such as:

Line pipe for upstream gathering, refineries, and high-pressure transmission to standard API 5L.

Piles, casing, and structural tubulars for bridges, high-rises, and civil-engineering shells.

Water mains, storm drains, tunnels, and district networks that need long service life.

Process lines, cooling circuits, and plant piping where wall thickness and weld quality matter.

Low-pressure conveyance for irrigation laterals, drainage, and on-farm water distribution.

Irrigation systems
