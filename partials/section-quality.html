Focus areas

Dimensional inspection

Traceability

Laboratory testing

Documentation

Third-party inspection

Every pipe undergoes precise dimensional checks — diameter, wall thickness, length, and ovality — ensuring full compliance with project specifications.

Each pipe is assigned a unique identification code, linking it to raw material certificates, production data, and test results throughout its lifecycle.

In-house laboratory performs mechanical, chemical, and metallographic testing to verify material properties meet the required standards.

Comprehensive quality documentation — mill certificates, inspection reports, and compliance records — accompanies every delivery.

Independent inspectors verify production quality on-site, providing additional assurance for critical infrastructure projects.
